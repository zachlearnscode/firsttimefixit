    About

    Previous Next

    First Time Fix It was established in 2017 by Army veteran and certified mechanic John Garza.

    Upfront, honest and down-to-earth, John provides high-quality repairs with high-quality parts at a reasonable price.

    John is 2015 graduate of the Universal Technical Institute in Phoenix, Arizona, where he earned certification in automotive technology.

    John specializes in Ford, Acura/Honda and BMW vehicles but accepts all makes and models at the shop.

    He ensures the proper time is taken with every job to make sure each vehicle is accurately diagnosed and repaired without any surprises or comebacks.

    For your peace of mind, First Time Fix It provides a 12-month/12-000-mile, nationwide warranty with all of our repairs.

    At First Time Fix It, we fix it the first time.
